## 🚀 About Me

I am a Computer Science and Engineering student and active researcher building at the intersection of **Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and Cyber Security**. My GitHub serves as a portfolio of my technical journey, featuring various projects developed primarily in Python and C++ that range from intelligent model architectures to robust security implementations. 

- 🔭 **Currently focusing on:** Advanced ML models, deep learning architectures, and InfoSec.
- 💻 **Main toolkit:** Python, C++, and a vast array of modern frameworks.
- ♟️ **Beyond the screen:** I am a dedicated student of history and literature, and I find strategic inspiration in the game of chess.
- 💡 **My Philosophy:** *"The best solutions are built with the precision of an algorithm, the foresight of a chess grandmaster, and the contextual depth of a classic novel."*
